🚨 **Excited to share RapidAssist – our hackathon project built for INVENTO 2026!** 🚨

In an emergency, every second counts. That's the idea that drove our team to build **RapidAssist** – a full-stack emergency response and civic issue reporting platform that puts help just one tap away.

---

🔑 **What does RapidAssist do?**

✅ **One-tap SOS** – Citizens can trigger an emergency alert to Police 👮, Ambulance 🚑, or Fire 🚒 services with a single press. A 7-second countdown lets users cancel accidental triggers, while the app simultaneously captures GPS location, 5 photo frames, and a 7-second audio recording as evidence.

✅ **AI-Powered Civic Reporting** – Snap a photo of a pothole, broken streetlight, or water leak. Google Gemini AI automatically categorizes the issue, assigns a priority level, and routes it to the right authority – no manual tagging needed.

✅ **Real-Time Admin Dashboards** – Dedicated control centers for Police, Ambulance, Fire, and General Admin allow responders to monitor incoming emergencies live, review evidence, assign cases, and update statuses in real time.

✅ **Medical Profile & Smart Summaries** – Users can store blood group, medical conditions, and emergency contacts. During an SOS, Gemini generates a concise health summary so first responders arrive prepared.

✅ **Community Feed** – Browse, upvote, and track civic issues reported by neighbors. Full resolution lifecycle from submission to closure, all in one feed.

---

🛠️ **Tech Stack:**
- ⚛️ React 18 + Vite 5 (Frontend)
- 🔥 Firebase Realtime Database (Live sync)
- 🗺️ Leaflet Maps (Location tracking)
- 🤖 Google Gemini API (AI analysis)
- 🗄️ Supabase Storage + ImgBB (Evidence storage)
- ☁️ Deployed on Vercel

💡 This project taught our team how to balance speed-of-development with real-world reliability under hackathon pressure. Building a system where lives could genuinely depend on uptime pushed us to think about error handling, data consistency, and UX in ways we never had before.
